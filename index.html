About VVHP Agro Food LLP â€” NutriDry

VVHP Agro Food LLP is a farmer-forward enterprise focused on reducing post-harvest losses and delivering nutrient-dense, shelf-stable food solutions. Under the NutriDry brand, we turn surplus and seasonal produce into high-quality dehydrated powders that are convenient for homes, cafeterias, and food service businesses.

Mission

  • Empower smallholder farmers with fair procurement and steady demand.
  • Reduce food waste through gentle dehydration and cold-processing methods.
  • Provide accessible, nutrient-rich products that fit modern kitchens.

Vision

  • Be a trusted name in natural, traceable, and sustainable food ingredients.
  • Scale impact by training farmers and creating decentralised drying hubs.
  • Champion transparent supply chains and environmentally responsible packaging.
